How Tydes helps surfers

Tydes is being built as a surf-focused travel app where users can discover surf spots, track conditions, connect with other surfers, and find places to stay that match the kind of trip they want across Australia and Southeast Asia.

Wave information Browse spot discovery, skill level, forecast windows, tides, and live or estimated conditions in one place.
Community Post updates, build a profile, connect with friends, and follow the surf conversation around the places people are traveling.
Find stays View places to stay by surf area like Canggu, Uluwatu, Byron Bay, or Siargao instead of generic city search.
