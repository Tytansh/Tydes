What Tydes does

Tydes is being built as a surf-focused travel app where users can discover surf spots, explore destinations by area, and find places to stay that match the kind of trip they want across the main surf routes in Australia and Southeast Asia.

Surf spots Browse surf breaks, skill level, forecast windows, and tide context in one place.
Area-based stays View places to stay by surf area like Canggu, Uluwatu, Byron Bay, or Siargao instead of generic city search.
Trip planning Save favorite stays, compare areas, and build surf trips around where users want to live.
